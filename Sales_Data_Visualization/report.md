# Business Insights

1. Technology category generates highest sales.

2. West region contributes maximum revenue.

3. Sales increase during certain months.

4. Higher sales do not always lead to higher profit.

5. Some products generate losses despite strong sales.

# Business Story:

The company performs strongly in the Technology category,
which contributes the largest share of revenue.

However, profitability is uneven across categories.

Regional analysis shows that some regions drive a
disproportionate share of sales.

Sales trends indicate seasonal demand patterns,
which can help management optimize inventory planning.

The relationship between sales and profit suggests
that discounting may be reducing profitability for
certain products.
